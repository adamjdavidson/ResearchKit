ResearchKit helps researchers, consultants, and writers conduct systematic research that combines:
- **Rigorous methodology** (constitutional research principles)
- **Multi-disciplinary perspectives** (research streams)
- **Vivid storytelling** (narrative evidence library)

## ğŸ§¬ Core Philosophy

### Constitutional Research
Like constitutional software development, ResearchKit operates on **immutable methodological principles** that ensure quality, rigor, and actionable outputs.

### Multi-Perspective Analysis
Complex questions require multiple disciplinary lenses. ResearchKit enforces examination through at least 3 different perspectives.

### Narrative Evidence
Abstract concepts become memorable when paired with vivid, character-driven stories. ResearchKit treats stories as first-class research artifacts.

### Practitioner Readiness
Research outputs must be actionable. ResearchKit applies practical relevance filters throughout the workflow.
